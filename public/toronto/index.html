Why Toronto CISOs Choose CISO Open Mic

At CISO Open Mic Toronto, the mic belongs to the community. No vendor pitches, no scripted panels, no moderators. Just you, your peers, and the mic — a safe space for Toronto CISOs to talk through the realities of leadership in cybersecurity.
