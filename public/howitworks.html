How CISO Open Mic Works

The Format

CISO Open Mic is an interactive evening where security leaders share stories, lessons, and questions with peers. No slide decks — just candid insights.

Participation

  • Anyone can step up to the mic — share a challenge, lesson, or ask a question.
  • Short, focused shares keep the pace high and the conversation lively.
  • Moderation ensures respectful, on-topic dialogue.

Who Should Attend

  • CISOs and senior security leaders
  • Security architects and program owners
  • Trusted sponsors and community partners

Code of Conduct

We foster a respectful, private environment. Be constructive, protect confidentiality, and avoid sales pitches from the mic.
